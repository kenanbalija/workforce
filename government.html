We make it a great day for you and your team.

WFS specializes in the training needs for the six (6) different employee categories or groups identified by OSHA Federal Employee Occupational Safety and Health (FEOSH) standard at 29 CFR Part 1960.

  • Management officials, 1960.54,
  • Supervisory employees, 1960.55
  • Safety and health specialists, 1960.56
  • Safety and health inspectors, 1960.57
  • Collateral duty employees & safety and health committee members, 1960.58
  • Employees & employee representatives, 1960.59
